Sensor vs Intuitive

  		•

    • Sensing vs. Intuition:
      • Perception and Gathering Information
        Intuitive Trust info that is theoretical, abstract
        Think in associations in a wider context, allusions
        Trust flashes of insight bubbling up from unconscious
        Meaning is in how the data relates to pattern or theory
        Sensor Trust info that is present, tangible, concrete
        Info should be understood by 5 senses
        Distrust hunches seemingly out of nowhere: prefer detail and facts.
        Meaning is in the data
