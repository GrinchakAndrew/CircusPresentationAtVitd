Thinking vs. Feeling

  		•

    • Feeling vs. Thinking:
      • Decision-making (Judging) Functions
        Feeling Associate with the situation, looking at it “from the inside”
        Try to achieve harmony and balance
        Consensus is the goal
        Consider the needs of all the people involved
        Thinking Prefer to decide things from a more detached standpoint
        Measure decision by reason and logics
        Demarcation is the goal
        Try to conform to a given set of rules
